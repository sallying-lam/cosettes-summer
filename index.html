🇨🇳 中文創意小寫作

寫作與分析
主題與格式

四格漫畫看圖作文：《拾金不昧》

寫作結構要領：六何法 + 描寫細節
誰 (Who)
何時 (When)
何地 (Where)
何事 (What)
為何 (Why)
結果 (How)
🧠 心理描寫至少一句： 例如：我的心像十五個吊桶打水——七上八下。
🌳 環境描寫至少一句： 例如：金燦燦的陽光透過樹葉灑落一地。

✍️ English Storytelling Lab

Composition
Topic 2

The Owl Who Lost Its Hoot 🦉

🎯 Learning Goals for this story:

1. **Expressing complex emotions:** Use words representing subtle emotions (embarrassed, anxious, hopeful).

2. **Structuring resolutions:** Leave plenty of time for a fully developed ending instead of rushing it!

Required Elements Self Check
⭐ Divided cleanly into at least 3 distinct paragraphs 📄
⭐ Includes at least 3 advanced feeling words ❤️
⭐ Descriptive ending showing the forest environment 🌲
